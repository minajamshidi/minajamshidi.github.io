This is an M.Sc. module in the winter semester 2023/2024 (October 2023 - April 2024) at Technical University Berlin. 

In this module, students will implement a scientific paper completely and reproduce their results. This includes implementations in Python using one of the modern deep learning frameworks (e.g., Pytorch). 


Responsibilities: 
* Supervising students for their implementation tasks, correcting and giving feedback to students on their coding and reports.
